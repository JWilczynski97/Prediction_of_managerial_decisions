    Team News

    Paris Saint-Germain
    • Serge Aurier may drop to the bench after his glaring error in the weekend 1-1 draw with Toulouse.
    • Ezequiel Lavezzi, Zlatan Ibrahimovic and captain Thiago Silva will all miss out.
    • Lucas Moura is expected to start alongside Edinson Cavani and Javier Pastore in attack.
    Barcelona
    • Luis Suárez is suspended for Barcelona, though his return is rapidly approaching.
    • Jordi Masip is injured and will miss the trip to France and is Luis Enrique's only injury concern.
    • Enrique should name a more experienced squad for the trip to PSG.

    Prediction

    • PSG are enduring a poor run of form at present and Laurent Blanc needs a win here in order to alleviate the pressure mounting on his job. Injury to star striker Zlatan Ibrahimovic won't help the Frenchman's cause.
    • Barcelona have begun to find their form this season and the 6-0 thumping of Granada puts them top of La Liga. If the Catalan side can transfer their league form into Europe, they will have a very good chance of winning the competition this time around.
    • The hosts know 3 points are a must after the 1-1 draw with Ajax last time out, but Barcelona could sneak this.
