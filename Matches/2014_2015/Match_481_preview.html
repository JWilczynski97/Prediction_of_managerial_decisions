    Team News

    Barcelona
    • Sergio Busquets and Thomas Vermaelen remain the only absentees to Luis Enrique through injury.
    • Dani Alves and Jordi Alba should return to the side in their usual full-back roles having missed out at the weekend through suspension.
    • The likes of Mascherano, Iniesta and Xavi were all on the bench for the 2-0 win over Eibar.
    Manchester City
    • Yaya Toure returns from suspension but Gael Clichy is out after his red card in the first leg at the Etihad.
    • After their humiliating defeat to Burnley all but handed the Premier League title to Chelsea, Manuel Pellegrini's long-term future at the club may yet rely on his side's performance at Camp Nou.
    • With that in mind he may ditch a 4-4-2 formation that has been criticised of late, with the likes of Nasri and Milner in line for a return.

    Prediction

    • Barcelona hold a strong advantage going into the second leg knowing that City must score at least twice to have any chance of progressing and they should be more wary of the counter attack than usual in turn.
    • Manuel Pellegrini must set his side up to score goals while remaining compact enough to not leave Barcelona's front three the space to pick up the ball. When you put it like that it seems a near-impossible task.
    • The Chilean's job is now far from secure and this will provide a test as to whether his players are truly up for the fight to ease the pressure on the manager. Either way the visitors have likely left themselves too much to do.
