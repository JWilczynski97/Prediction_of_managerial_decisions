    Team News

    Chelsea
    • Nemanja Matic somehow managed to pick up an injury in the celebrations after the Captial One Cup victory at Wembley, but there is very little chance indeed that he will miss this match as a result.
    • John Obi Mikel is still out.
    • Oscar has been lacking form of late and could be dropped to the bench with Ramires adding an extra body in central midfield and Cesc Fabregas playing further forward.
    Paris Saint-Germain
    • Yohan Cabaye is nearing a return from a thigh injury but won't go straight into the team even if he is passed fit.

    Prediction

    • After a first leg 1-1 draw, this tie is finely balanced ahead of the return match at Stamford Bridge on Wednesday night. Chelsea come into the game after a week's rest, and certainly shouldn't have any issues motivating themselves after their recent triumph at Wembley.
    • PSG thumped Lens 4-1 at the weekend with a rotated team, and will come flying out of the blocks here as they seek the goal they need to have any chance of progressing.
    • Chelsea will soak up the pressure and will be happy to spend most of the match in their own half, before looking to strike on the counter. The Blues are ruthless winners in important matches and should manage another victory here.
