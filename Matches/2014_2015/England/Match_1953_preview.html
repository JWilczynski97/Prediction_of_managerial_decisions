    Prediction

    • The North London Derby kicks off this weekend's action, with both sides coming into the game on decent enough bouts of form: in a Premier League table based on each team's last 6 matches, Spurs and Arsenal are third and second, respectively.
    • Form can, however, mean little in games of this magnitude, and with only two points separating the sides there is even greater significance in this one.
    • Tottenham will again look to Harry Kane and Christian Eriksen to inspire them to victory, while Arsenal have incredible strength in attacking positions and thus have more chance of changing the game with substitutions as the game wears on.
    • The Gunners have looked resurgent of late and could well be strong enough to win a second successive time at White Hart Lane.
