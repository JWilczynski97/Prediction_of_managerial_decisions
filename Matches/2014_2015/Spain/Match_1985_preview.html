    Team News

    Barcelona
    Almeria
    • Javier Mascherano will come straight back into the side in defensive midfield after suspension, replacing Sergio Busquets who will sit out due to an accumulation of yellow cards.
    • Jordi Alba is a continued injury absentee, with Adriano continuing at left-back as a result. Martin Montoya and Marc Bartra are also in contention for a start. After returning to training in recent weeks, Thomas Vermaelen is in with a chance of featuring.
    • Pedro could be inserted into the forward line after a rather poor performance from Barca's offensive options against Celta Vigo, while Xavi should get a run out from the start in midfield.
    • Fran Velez joins Michel Macedo as an injury absentee, with suspensions for Verza, Maruo Dos Santos and Sebastian Dubarbier meaning that there is something of a shake up for Juan Ignacio Martinez's side.
    • Thomas Partey returns from suspension and should come straight back into midfield, with Ramon Azeez a likely partner for the former Atletico man.
    • Tommer Hemed might drop out of the 11, with Thievy Bifouma likely to be preferred up top, while Jonathan and Edgar provide ammunition from the wide areas.

    • Almeria are on a run of seven La Liga games without a win, with only two away victories to their name in 2015 thus far. As the visitors currently sit a point from safety, points are needed from any contest regardless of opposition.
