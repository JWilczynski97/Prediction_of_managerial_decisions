    Prediction

    • It has been an unsettling week for Barcelona. Lionel Messi's declarations in Manchester triggered a whirlwind of rumours and speculation that the four-time Ballon d'Or winner is planning to leave Camp Nou. Luis Enrique will have to be at his man-managing best to deal with the situation and have his players fully focused on this game.
    • Barcelona have flattered to deceive of late. They have suffered two losses in their last three Liga games and their one win, which was against Almeria, was hardly convincing. Yet, the performances of Luis Suarez are slowly improving and offering more encouragement. The former Liverpool hitman has failed to open his account for his new club and it is only a matter of time before he does.
    • The Catalans record against Sevilla is impressive; it is five wins on the trot and now they are going for number six. Indeed, the last time Sevilla got the better of the Azulgranas at Camp Nou was way back in 2002, when they won 0-3.
    • The return of Ivan Rakitic against his former employers is an interesting subplot. The Croatian has divided opinion since his move to Catalunya so he will be extra motivated to lift his game for this one.
