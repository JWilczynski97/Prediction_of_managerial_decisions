    Team News

    Barcelona
    • Dani Alves returns from suspension and will be expected to start at right-back for the Spanish side.
    • Andres Iniesta is a doubt for the hosts and may miss out, with Xavi and Rafinha battling for a starting spot.
    • Thomas Vermaelen once more misses out.
    Paris Saint-Germain
    • Serge Aurier will miss out once more through suspension, with fellow defender Thiago Silva also missing out for the French side.
    • Gregory Van der Wiel is expected to keep his place in the starting XI, with Marquinhos once again partnering David Luiz at the heart of the backline.
    • Zlatan Ibrahimovic will likely line up against his former employers, though Thiago Motta misses out through injury.

    Prediction

    • Barcelona all-but secured their spot in the semi final of the Champions League with a comprehensive 3-1 win over PSG in Paris last week. Luis Enrique will demand his players focus on the task at hand, but they have an overwhelming advantage coming into this fixture.
    • PSG have a number of key players returning to first team action, but face an uphill struggle to secure a place in the next round of the competition. The French side crumbled on home turf and are expected to leave Camp Nou empty handed on Tuesday night.
    • Barcelona may have struggled in periods at the weekend, but managed to secure a 2-0 home win over Valencia. A similar result is likely here.
