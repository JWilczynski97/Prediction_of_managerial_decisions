    Team News

    Leicester
    Liverpool
    • Managerless Leicester City take on Liverpool after sacking Claudio Ranieri on Thursday and some changes are expected to the side. Demarai Gray is one of those that should be reinstalled into the starting XI in favour of Ahmed Musa.
    • Islam Slimani and Leonardo Ulloa weren't fit enough for the 2-1 defeat to Sevilla on Wednesday and may not recover in time for the reception of Liverpool on Monday.
    • Daniel Sturridge returned from Liverpool's mid-season training camp in La Manga early due to a virus but should be fit enough to make the trip to Leicester on Sunday.
    • Jordan Henderson is ruled out with a foot injury and will be replaced in the team by Emre Can.
    • Dejan Lovren was sent to Germany for further treatment on an on-going knee problem during the week and he is expected to miss out here, with Ragnar Klavan or Lucas Leiva poised to deputise in his absence.

    Prediction

    • Leicester are only one point above the bottom three and the board have made the decision to sack Claudio Ranieri less than 12 months after they had won the Premier League title. They have lost seven of their last nine matches in all competitions and now have 13 league games to stay in the Premier League.
    • Liverpool have had two weeks to prepare for this fixture and they should be ready to go following their 2-0 win over Tottenham last time out. Jurgen Klopp will hope their slump is over and they can get their season back on track and finish inside the top four.
    • It remains to be seen how Leicester will react to Ranieri's sacking, but Liverpool should still have enough to win this match.
