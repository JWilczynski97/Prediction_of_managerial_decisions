    Caen

    • Despite being involved in the third goal against Nancy after coming off the bench, Mathieu Valbuena, Jordan Ferri and Clment Grenier might have to miss out on the starting XI once more.

    Prediction

    • Alexandre Lacazette's hat-trick against Nancy at the weekend gave his side a chance to quickly move on from their Super Cup defeat to PSG. With their confidence back, les Gones will now be eager to extend their lead on top of the division by winning in Friday's early fixture.
    • Caen were 2-0 down after only 18 minutes in Saturday's clash against Lorient when FCL centre-back Zargo Tour's red card provoked a much-needed reaction within the ranks, which ultimately saw new signing Ivan Santini net a late winner. Patrice Garande's side cannot afford to repeat the same mistakes against Lyon this weekend, however, as they meet with a side who have never met their match at the Parc OL thus far.
    • Lyon must build on last weekend's achievement against Caen next up, who they comfortably beat on two occasions last season.
