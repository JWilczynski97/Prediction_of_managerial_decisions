    Team News

    Bayern Munich
    • Mats Hummels is ruled out for Bayern, which should see Jerome Boateng partner up with Javi Martinez once more.
    • Philipp Lahm returns from suspension having missed the second leg win over Arsenal last time out in the Champions League.
    • Manuel Neuer, Thomas Muller and Douglas Costa are all doubts, though the former is in line to return here, while Robert Lewandowski misses out with a shoulder problem.
    Real Madrid
    • Real Madrid have a minor defensive crisis, with Raphael Varane and Pepe both ruled out.
    • Nacho should start alongside Sergio Ramos at centre-back, but the rest of the XI picks itself.
    • Fabio Coentrao will hope to be involved, while Dani Carvajal is a serious doubt having missed training earlier this week.

    Prediction

    • Bayern geared up for the welcome of Real Madrid in the best way possible having dispatched of Borussia Dortmund 4-1 on Saturday. They have been in flying form on home turf of late, winning their last seven and conceding just twice in that run.
    • Away from home, Real Madrid have won their last five, though were held to a 1-1 draw with rivals Atletico Madrid at the weekend. They secured a 4-0 win over the Bundesliga giants the last time these met at the Allianz Arena in the Champions League, though the hosts come into this clash as favourites.
    • This is expected to be a close encounter, but the hosts have the means to edge victory here.
