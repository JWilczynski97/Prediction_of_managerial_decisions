    Team News

    Real Madrid
    • Real Madrid tested a back three at the weekend but looked far more comfortable when they reverted back to a four-man defence midway through their 3-1 win away to Osasuna.
    • Gareth Bale has returned to training after more than two months out injured but won't come into consideration until the second leg at the start of March.
    Napoli
    • Lorenzo Tonelli is the only injury concern for Napoli, while Kalidou Koulibaly is one booking away from a ban.

    Prediction

    • Real Madrid aren't playing at their best but have got that winning feeling back and are favourites to overcome Napoli over the two legs.
    • Napoli are arguably one of the most enjoyable teams to watch in Europe this season. They haven't lost a game since October and have won 11 of their last 13 in all competitions.
    • The Serie A side are unbeaten away from home in the Champions League this season and definitely have what it takes to cause an upset here. They scored seven goals the last time they played away from home, and while that isn't going to happen here, they have enough to cause Madrid a lot of problems.
