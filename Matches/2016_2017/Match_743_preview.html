    Team News

    Atletico Madrid
    • Diego Godin was sent off at the weekend but his league suspension will not stop him from starting against Real Madrid on Wednesday.
    • With Juanfran, Sime Vrsaljko and Jose Gimenez on the sidelines, Lucas Hernandez may have to play at right-back like he did in the first leg.
    Real Madrid
    • Zinedine Zidane can recall almost all of his usual starters for the second leg after resting his first-team at the weekend.
    • The only real question in the team is whether Zidane goes with Danilo or Nacho at right-back to cover for Dani Carvajal, who is out for the rest of the season.
    • Gareth Bale is also expected to miss out on Wednesday. Isco is expected to be given the nod over Alvaro Morata and James Rodriguez.

    Prediction

    • Atletico Madrid are facing the real possibility of elimination from the Champions League at the hands to Real Madrid once again. They lost 3-0 in the first leg and it would require a collapse of epic proportions for Madrid to throw this tie away now.
    • Madrid haven't haven't lost by 3+ goals without scoring themselves in the Champions League since going down 4-0 away to Liverpool in March 2009. Diego Simeone's side did win 4-0 at home to Madrid in February 2015, but it's hard to see a fresh Zidane side succumbing to a similar defeat this time around.
