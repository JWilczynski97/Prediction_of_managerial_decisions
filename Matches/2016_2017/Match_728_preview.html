    Team News

    Leicester
    • Robert Huth and Islam Slimani are both one booking away from a suspension in UEFA competition. No major changes are expected from Craig Shakespeare, with Islam Slimani still a doubt and Nampalys Mendy sidelined.
    Sevilla
    • Sevilla are able to switch between a variety of formations so it remains to be seen what system Jorge Sampaoli goes for against Leicester on Tuesday.
    • Vicente Iborra and Nicols Pareja are a booking away from a suspension, while Gabriel Mercado returns from a one-match ban.

    Prediction

    • Leicester have won back-to-back matches for the first time since October after sacking Claudio Ranieri and promoting Craig Shakespeare to first-team manager. They are still in this tie after earning a crucial away goal in a 2-1 defeat out in Sevilla and should draw on the fact they have a 100% home record in the Champions League this season.
    • Sevilla are unbeaten in their last eight matches in all competitions but have drawn their last two matches and are now out of the title race in La Liga. While they haven't conceded away from home in the Champions League this season, they have only managed to find the back of the net once themselves.
    • Leicester are the underdogs but should sense a real opportunity to pull off a major upset on Tuesday night.
