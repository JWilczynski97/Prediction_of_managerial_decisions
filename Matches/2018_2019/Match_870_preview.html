    Team News

    Barcelona
    • Few surprises are expected from Barcelona when they host PSV in their first Champions League game of the season.
    • Philippe Coutinho will make his Champions League debut for Barcelona this week.
    PSV Eindhoven
    • PSV will be without Ryan Thomas, Dante Rigo and Maximiliano Romero for Tuesday's trip to Camp Nou. The Dutch giants will field their strongest side.

    Prediction

    • Barcelona have won their Champions League group in each of the past 11 seasons. They have started the season with a 100 per cent record.
    • PSV have won seven games in a row and have scored 13 goals in their last two outings.
    • The Dutch giants, however, haven't win away in the Champions League proper since November 2007, losing seven of their subsequent 10 matches.
    • Barcelona are unbeaten in 26 Champions League home matches and are expected to continue that streak on Tuesday.
