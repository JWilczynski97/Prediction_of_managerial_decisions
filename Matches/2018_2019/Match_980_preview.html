    Team News

    Barcelona
    • Rafinha is out for the season for the home side, while Ousmane Dembele and Thomas Vermaelen will be assessed ahead of Barcelona's welcome of Lyon.
    • Should Dembele fail to prove his fitness, Coutinho could be handed another chance to impress on the left.
    • Arturo Vidal and Arthur are competing for a starting spot alongside Ivan Rakitic and Sergio Busquets in midfield.
    Lyon
    • Nabil Fekir is available following suspension, which is a huge boost for Lyon as they aim to upset the odds in Barcelona.
    • Lyon are sweating on the fitness of Rafael and Marcelo, however, though the latter should be fine to start here.

    Prediction

    • Barcelona will be hoping their profligacy from the first leg doesn't come back to haunt them when they welcome Lyon to Camp Nou on Wednesday night. The hosts mustered 25 shots in the 0-0 draw, but have scored in every home game this season.
    • Lyon will be buoyed by the first leg result, but away from home, they have failed to win in three. That being said, a score draw would see the French side progress to the next round, so if they can nick an early goal, then they'll be confident of securing a quarter-final spot.
    • However, Barcelona should secure the result needed to progress.
