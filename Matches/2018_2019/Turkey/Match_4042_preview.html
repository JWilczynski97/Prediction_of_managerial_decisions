    Prediction

    • Hard work, solid defending, and crowding out their opponents in midfield have brought Antalyaspor recent success. They were relegation contenders at the beginning of the season but they start the weekend in fourth.
    • Antalyaspor manager Korkmaz knows Terim too well having played under him for years, including a UEFA Cup success in 2000, and has declared his intentions to go out and record a memorable win. His side will leave no space in their own half and use Maicon and Doukara's pace to create counter-attacking opportunities.
    • Galatasaray's away performances have been less than impressive so far. They have been unable to dominate games, dictate tempo, create chances and have suffered from lapses of concentration in defence. Their performance against Porto was better but many believe that the players are picking what games to perform in, rather than a sign of general improvement.
    • Galatasaray would usually be favourites but, after a tough midweek trip to Portugal, Antalyaspor will fancy their chances of getting at least a point here.
