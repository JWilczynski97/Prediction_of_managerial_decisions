    Paris Saint-Germain
    • Edinson Cavani and Neymar continue to miss out for Paris Saint-Germain.
    • Thomas Tuchel may go with the same starting XI as the first leg, though Christopher Nkunku could replace Dani Alves on the right flank.
    • Alphonse Areola is reportedly tipped to start in goal on Wednesday, having watched the first leg from the bench.
    • Julian Draxler, Thilo Kehrer, Marco Verratti and Juan Bernet are one booking away from missing their next Champions League game.
    Manchester United
    • Manchester United are 2-0 down from the first leg and will likely be without 10 first-team players in Paris.
    • Paul Pogba is suspended after being sent off in the first game and his absence will all but end any slim hopes United had of overturning the deficit.
    • Alexis Sanchez added to United's injury woes over the weekend and could now miss up to two months with knee ligament damage.
    • Eric Bailly started the first leg against PSG and may be drafted into the team here to try and combat Kylian Mbappe's pace, though that didn't work at Old Trafford.
    • Anthony Martial is the closest of United's injured players to return but Ole Gunnar Solskjaer has not risked the Frenchman here.
    • Ashley Young is one booking away from a one-match ban in European competition.
