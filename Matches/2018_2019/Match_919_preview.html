    Team News

    Monaco
    • Pele was sent off in Monaco's 1-0 defeat to Reims at the weekend but he is available for Thierry Henry's side in midweek.
    • Monaco have endured a nightmare in goal this season but Diego Benaglio is at least free to start here, with Danijel Subasic on his way back from injury.
    • Appointing Henry as manager has brought about no improvements in terms of results or points and he still has huge injury problems to contend with.
    • The Monaco boss can at least call on Radamel Falcao again after the Colombian recovered from injury to feature from the bench against Reims.
    Club Brugge
    • Jelle Vossen picked up a knee injury midway through October and is expected to miss the rest of the group stage.
    • Arnaut Danjuma Groeneveld has not played since suffering an ankle injury against Monaco last time out in the competition.
    • Siebe Schrijvers has scored six goals in his last six appearances and should replace Vossen in attack for the away side.
    • Marvelous Nakamba and Mats Rits are in competition to start in midfield.

    Prediction

    • Thierry Henry has made a dreadful start to his managerial career and says the Monaco players lacked desire in their latest defeat. Henry is winless through his first four matches in charge of the French side (D2, L2).
    • Club Bruges are unbeaten in their last five matches, but have drawn four of those, including a 1-1 stalemate with Monaco last time out in the Champions League.
    • Monaco are without a win in 11 European games and they have lost their last five at home.
    • Club Bruges, meanwhile, are without a win in their last 13 Champions League fixtures, qualifying included (D2, L11).
    • Neither side are in good form in the competition and another draw could be on the cards on Tuesday.
