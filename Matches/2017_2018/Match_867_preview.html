    Team News

    Real Madrid
    • Daniel Carvajal has been been ruled out for Real Madrid due to an injury picked up in the first leg, while Isco is a huge doubt.
    • Nacho returned to training at the start of the week but hasn't played in more a month and will not be at 100% here.
    • With a lack of other alternatives at right-back, Lucas Vázquez may have to fill in again, as he did at the end of the first leg.
    • Cristiano Ronaldo and Sergio Ramos were given the weekend off, while Raphael Varane has a slight problem and was not risked.
    • Karim Benzema and Gareth Bale were only named on the bench in the first leg but Zinedine Zidane may call on one of them here.
    Bayern Munich
    • Jérôme Boateng and Arjen Robben both miss out for Bayern Munich after picking up injuries in the first leg.
    • Niklas Süle is expected to deputise for Boateng, while Thiago Alcantara will hope to profit from Robben's expected absence.
    • Kingsley Coman, Manuel Neuer and Arturo Vidal remain sidelined for the away side.
    • David Alaba has travelled with the squad after missing the first leg through injury and could start at left-back on Tuesday.
    • Jupp Heynckes fielded a reserve side at the weekend and will recall on his strongest available players on Tuesday night.

    Prediction

    • Real Madrid are in the driving seat in this Champions League semi-final, but as they showed in the last round, they can not take any lead for granted.
    • Bayern Munich are down but certainly not out. Juventus beat Madrid at the Bernabéu in the last round and Bayern have to believe they can do the same.
    • Do Madrid have Munich's number at the moment? The reigning European champions have won each of the last four meetings between the two teams.
