    Team News

    Bayern Munich
    • Manuel Neuer is expected to miss out for Bayern Munich here.
    • Thiago returned to first-team action at the weekend after nearly three months out injured and he will hope to start here.
    • Joshua Kimmich, Corentin Tolisso and Sebastian Rudy will miss the return leg in Turkey if they are booked on Tuesday.
    Besiktas
    • Alvaro Negredo or Vagner Love will lead the line for Besiktas following the departure of Cenk Tosun in January.
    • Tolgay Arslan, Caner Erkin and Anderson Talisca will miss the second leg if they are booked on Tuesday.

    Prediction

    • Bayern Munich have won their last 13 matches in all competitions and are unbeaten at home this season, albeit only keeping one clean sheet in their last seven on home soil.
    • Besiktas have only lost two of their last 24 competitive matches and are unbeaten in seven ahead of Tuesday's daunting clash with Bayern. Converting draws into wins has proved a real problem for Besiktas this season, but they would take that here.
    • Bayern are expected to win on Tuesday but their home defensive frailties should give a Besiktas side that have scored in all but one away game this season hope.
