    Team News

    Napoli
    • Kalidou Koulibaly is suspended for the hosts while Dries Mertens is one yellow card away from his own one-match ban.
    • Arkadiusz Milik and Faouzi Ghoulam are the only long-term concerns for Napoli, but Lorenzo Insigne is also a major knock after suffering a knee injury at the weekend.
    • Maurizio Sarri could make some changes to his midfield three with the likes of Amadou Diawara and Piotr Zielinski pushing for game time.
    Shakhtar Donetsk
    • Yaroslav Rakitskiy is available again after missing their last Champions League outing through suspension, but Sergey Krivtsov and Maksim Malyshev are still sidelined.

    Prediction

    • Shakhtar Donetsk can join Manchester City in the knockout phase of the Champions League if they avoid defeat against Napoli on Tuesday. The away side are currently six points clear of Napoli in Group G but have to face City in their last game.
    • Napoli need to win their last two matches to have any chance of going through after only managing one win from their first four matches. It would be a major disappointment for the Italian side if they failed to make the next round, but it looks that way at the moment.
