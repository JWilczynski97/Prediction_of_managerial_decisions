    Team News

    Man City
    Liverpool
    • Kyle Walker returns from suspension but Manchester City have will not have Raheem Sterling available after he was sent off before the international break.
    • Pep Guardiola has confirmed Vincent Kompany will not play against Liverpool after returning from the international break with another calf injury. His absence could force City to change formations.
    • Reverting to a back four could result in one of Gabriel Jesus or Sergio Aguero dropping to the bench against Liverpool, unless Jesus moves out wide.
    • Guardiola says Ilkay Gundogan will be part of Saturday's squad, but it's unlikely he will start.
    • Philippe Coutinho is not fit enough to feature and has been left out of the Liverpool squad for Saturday's trip to the Etihad.
    • Liverpool signed Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain from Arsenal towards the end of the transfer window but he is unlikely to start here.
    • Simon Mignolet was rested in Liverpool's last match before the international break and will be pushing to get the No.1 jersey back this weekend.
    • With Nathaniel Clyne out injured until the end of the year, Joe Gomez and Trent Alexander-Arnold continue to battle for a starting berth at right-back.
    • Klopp also has a decision to make about who starts at left-back between Alberto Moreno and Andrew Robertson.

    Prediction

    • Manchester City and Liverpool both have the opportunity to go top of the Premier League when they meet in the first fixture of the weekend, although it might only be for a matter of hours. This fixture usually produces entertaining matches, particularly now Jurgen Klopp and Pep Guardiola in the opposing dugouts.
    • Klopp is unbeaten in his last three matches against Guardiola-managed sides and has actually won five of their 10 previous meetings. He is one of very few managers that regularly gets the better of the Spaniard and we have another fascinating fixture on our hands this weekend.
    • City have only lost one home league match since Guardiola was appointed manager over 12 months ago and also drew this corresponding fixture 1-1 last season. Neither defences are particuarly solid and another entertaining fixture should be on the cards.
