    Team News

    Brighton
    Man City
    • Beram Kayal is ruled out for a number of months after breaking his leg, while Sam Baldock will also miss Brighton's first ever Premier League game through injury.
    • With Baldock out injured, Davy Propper or Izzy Brown may support Glenn Murray in attack.
    • Anthony Knockaert and Glenn Murray stepped up their returns with minutes for the U23s and will both be desperate to start against City. Chris Hughton has hinted Knockaert may only make the bench.
    • Solly March is expected to recover from a knee injury in time and will be available for the home side.
    • Benjamin Mendy is suffering with a thigh injury and that might delay his Manchester City debut. Danilo, who also joined City this summer, may have to deputise at left-back.
    • Ilkay Gndogan is back in training but hasn't featured since December and is expected to miss the first few weeks of the season as he gets back up to speed.
    • Pep Guardiola has a number of choices in midfield and attack and is expected to rotate between them all throughout the season. He will do very well to keep everyone happy at the club.

    Prediction

    • Brighton have been thrown in right at the deep end as they begin their first ever season in the Premier League as they host Manchester City on Saturday.
    • City are favourites for the Premier League title after spending more than 200m on new players this season. Pep Guardiola endured his first ever trophyless campaign last season and will be eyeing a good start.
    • The away side have so many world class players in attack that this could quite easily get messy for Brighton. You have the fear for the Premier League new boys this weekend.
