    Team News

    Chelsea
    • Chelsea manager Antonio Conte will have to decide whether to recall Alvaro Morata to the starting XI following his return to fitness or stick with an in-form Olivier Giroud.
    • Conte has revealed Marcos Alonso will be fine to face Barcelona after a three-game absence, but Tiemou Bakayoko is unlikely to recover in time.
    • Gary Cahill and Antonio Rudiger are in competition for a starting berth in Chelsea's back three on Tuesday.
    • David Luiz and Ross Barkley are also doubts for the home side but neither would have started if fit.
    Barcelona
    • Philippe Coutinho is ineligible for Barcelona after featuring in the group stage for Liverpool.
    • Ousmane Dembl is fit enough to travel to London and will compete with Paulinho for a start on the right flank.
    • Thomas Vermaelen will travel with the squad despite his injury concerns, but Nlson Semedo is suspended for the away side.

    Prediction

    • After a run of three defeats in four matches, Chelsea have won their last two matches by an aggregate scoreline of 7-0. The Blues have only lost two of 16 home matches against Spanish sides.
    • Barcelona are favourites for this season's Champions League and have only lost one game all season. Ernesto Valverde's side have kept clean sheets in four of their last five matches and are yet to concede away from home in the Champions League this season.
    • Lionel Messi has never scored against Chelsea (eight matches), which is his worst record against an opponent. He has only won once against the Premier League giants and that record will have to improve if Barcelona are to progress.
