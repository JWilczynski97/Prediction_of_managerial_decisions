    Team News

    Liverpool
    • Adam Lallana suffered a hamstring injury against Crystal Palace at the weekend and will not feature here as a result, while Emre Can is a huge doubt with a back problem.
    • Nathaniel Clyne hasn't featured all season but was named as a substitute against Palace. That is unlikely to change here.
    • Joel Matip is out for the rest of the season, meaning Dejan Lovren will partner Virgil Van Dijk at the back.
    • Alberto Moreno and Jordan Henderson will incur a one-match ban if they are booked on Wednesday night.
    Manchester City
    • Aymeric Laporte suffered a foot injury at the end of Manchester City's 3-1 win over Everton on Saturday but he will push to make an immediate return in midweek.
    • Pep Guardiola has confirmed Sergio Aguero is out of Wednesday's match, meaning Gabriel Jesus will lead the line for City at Anfield.
    • Concussion ruled John Stones out against his former club and he may miss out on another Merseyside return here, while Fabian Delph remains a doubt.
    • Danilo, Ilkay Gundogan and Fernandinho will incur a one-match ban with their next yellow cards.

    Prediction

    • Liverpool have only lost one of their 23 home matches this season and Jurgen Klopp's side beat Manchester City 4-3 at Anfield in January.
    • Pep Guardiola has lost more games against teams managed by Klopp than any other manager in his career (6 defeats).
    • Liverpool have kept clean sheets in their last three home matches. If they can continue that record against City then they stand a great chance of going through.
    • City, meanwhile, have kept clean sheets in three of their last four away games and have won those matches at an aggregate scoreline of 11-1.
    • However, defensive records could fly out of the window when these two come face-to-face on Wednesday.
