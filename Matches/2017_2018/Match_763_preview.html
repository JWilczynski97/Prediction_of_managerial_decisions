    Team News

    Borussia Dortmund
    • Borussia Dortmund will be without at least six players through injury for Tuesday's reception of reigning champions Real Madrid.
    • Marc Bartra came on for the final seven minutes of Dortmund's 6-1 win over Gladbach at the weekend, his first appearance in more than two weeks. He could replace Omer Toprak in defence here.
    • Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang has scored six goals in his last three matches and will be looking to prove Madrid wrong after failing to secure a move to Zidane's side over the summer.
    Real Madrid
    • Real Madrid will be without Karim Benzema, Jesus Vallejo, Marcelo, Theo Hernandez and Mateo Kovacic for the trip to Dortmund on Tuesday.
    • Nacho will continue at left-back with Marcelo and Hernandez sidelined through injury.
    • Toni Kroos is working hard to be fit in time for Tuesday's match but looks set to miss out.
    • Luka Modric was given the weekend off and will return to the starting XI against Dortmund.

    Prediction

    • Borussia Dortmund are flying after thrashing Bundesliga rivals Gladbach 6-1 at the weekend. This is probably the best time they will ever have to face Real Madrid, who have struggled so far this season.
    • Madrid have only lost one game this season but their performances haven't been anywhere near the standard expected from the reigning European champions. Zinedine Zidane's side have actually been better away from home, which may help them here.
    • After losing their first group match, a win for Dortmund would really blow this group wide open. One thing for certain should be goals: the last six encounters between the two sides have averaged 3.3 goals per game.
