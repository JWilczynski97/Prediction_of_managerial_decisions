    Team News

    Bayern Munich
    • Robert Lewandowski, Franck Ribery, Thomas Mller, Javi Martinez, Joshua Kimmich, and Mats Hummels will all return to the starting XI here after being held back at the weekend.
    • Kingsley Coman, Arturo Vidal and Manuel Neuer are all sidelined for Bayern Munich, while Rafinha is expected to start at left-back with David Alaba a doubt.
    Real Madrid
    • Real Madrid did not have a game over the weekend and will be well rested ahead of their trip to Germany on Wednesday.
    • Sergio Ramos is available again after seving a one-match ban in the competition, while Nacho has also returned to training.
    • Karim Benzema's form will be a cause for concern for Zinedine Zidane and could provide Gareth Bale with the chance to start up front.
    • Isco and Marco Asensio battle for a place in the starting XI behind the two strikers.

    Prediction

    • Bayern Munich are looking to make the Champions League final for the first time since 2013. In their way stand a Real Madrid side that are bidding to win the Champions League for a record third successive year.
    • Madrid have won the last five competitive meetings between the two sides and have to be favoured given the form of Cristiano Ronaldo. They have conceded in 10 of their last 12 away matches in the competition, however.
    • Bayern are undefeated at home this season, even keeping clean sheets in five of their last six. They have scored at least five goals in five of their last eight matches on home soil, but can they repeat such fine form against the reigning champions?
