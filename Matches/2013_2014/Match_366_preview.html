    Team News

    Real Madrid
    • Álvaro Arbeloa and Jesé will certainly miss out, while Sami Khedira isn't expected to feature despite returning to training.
    • Marcelo, Gareth Bale and Cristiano Ronaldo are doubts, though will be handed late fitness tests.
    • However, Carlo Ancelotti may be tempted to field a similar XI to the one that overcame Barcelona in the Copa Del Rey final last Wednesday.
    Bayern Munich
    • Bayern can welcome both Bastian Schweinsteiger and Javi Martínez back from suspension, though the latter is a doubt with a concussion.
    • Pep Guardiola is also sweating on the fitness of David Alaba, Manuel Neuer, Thomas Müller and Pierre-Emile Höjbjerg.
    • Holger Badstuber, Thiago Alcantara, Xherdan Shaqiri and Tom Starke will all miss out.

    Prediction

    • Wednesday night will be the 15th time both Real Madrid and Bayern Munich have met in the Champions League. The hosts will be looking to mark the occasion with a victory to take to the Allianz Arena. Los Blancos have won 15 of their last 17 Champions League meetings at the Bernabeu and should be confident of adding to this.
    • Bayern however have beaten Madrid 8 times in Europe's elite competition, more than any other team. The German champions have come through a questionable run of form and recently secured their place in the final of the DFB-Pokal, though they succumbed to a morale sapping 3-0 defeat to rivals Borussia Dortmund recently.
    • The hosts may be without the services of Gareth Bale through, but have proven they can win without him. This promises to be an entertaining affair with goals at either end.
