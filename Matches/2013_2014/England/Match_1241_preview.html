    Prediction

    • United have won each of their last 6 matches at Old Trafford against Liverpool and the two sides haven't drawn a match in Manchester since 2000.
    • The home fans will detest seeing the Reds fighting for the title while their hopes likely lie on a Europa League berth, and they'll be keen for revenge after the reverse fixture this season.
