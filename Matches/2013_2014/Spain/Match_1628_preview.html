    Prediction

    • Celta Vigo's results on the road strangely been significantly better than results at home. If only away matches were considered, the Galicians would be seventh in the table. Despite their strength away from home, playing at Camp Nou is a different proposition and they will find the going extremely tough. In Luis Enrique, Celta Vigo do have a coach who knows Barcelona very well and this will aid their cause.
    • Barcelona got themselves back into the title race with a sensational win over Real Madrid on Sunday night. The victory will give them a huge boost in confidence as they bid to retain their league title. At full strength and with Messi firing on all cylinders, expect the Catalans to find the net on a couple of occasions, if not more.
    • Neymar is likely to be dropped again for the visit of Celta. The Brazilian failed to shine at the Bernabeu on Sunday night and a fresher Pedro or Alexis Sanchez will likely take his place up front. No matter if it is the former or latter, the duo will pose a significant threat to Celta. Both will be itching to make an impression after being snubbed for the Clásico. Cesc Fábregas is another who could see himself return to the bench. The former Arsenal man was the other Barca player who did not play to the level expected of him.
