    Team News

    Real Madrid
    Sevilla
    • There is great news this week for Real Madrid with Xabi Alonso expected to return to action after his long lay-off.
    • Carlo Ancelotti will likely adopt the cautious approach and start the former Liverpool player on the bench.
    • The Italian tactician surprised everyone during the weekend's Clasico with his tactical and personnel tinkering. Expect him to revert to a more familiar eleven for the visit of Sevilla.
    • Gareth Bale, who started against Barca but is still not fully fit, will most likely return to the bench while Karim Benzema will get the nod up front.
    • Isco could return to the team also, possibly lining up alongside Luka Modric and Asier Illarramendi, who is tipped to return after sitting out on the weekend.
    • Ivan Rakitic returned to the side over the weekend and showed just why he was so missed during his injury lay-off. The Croatian put in a man-of-the-match performance, scoring one and leading his side to all three points over a stubborn Osasuna outfit.
    • Kevin Gameiro also returned from injury to play against the Pamplonans, but it was clear he was not fully fit and he could start this one from the bench with Diego Perotti taking his place.
    • Unai Emery praised his side's performance but lamented the goal they conceded, saying: "We have to be more solid at the back".

    • After their controversial loss to Barcelona, Real Madrid simply need to get all three points in order to prevent their rivals from extending their lead in the race for the title.
