    Prediction

    • Real Madrid are in their best form of the season. A resounding 5-0 defeat over rivals Atletico Madrid across their two-legged victory in the Copa del Rey has shown this, while last weekend they comfortably defeated Villarreal, despite conceding twice.
    • Carlo Ancelotti has done a great job with his side. It took them some time to get used to the Italian's ideas, but the side look and play completely different to how they did under Jose Mourinho. They now play with the ball and always look to attack, though they still are lightning fast on the counter-attack. In short, they are dangerous at all times and with the defence being tightened up, they are also extremely hard to score against.
    • Getafe have traditionally made life hard for their more illustrious city-rivals and actually beat them in this fixture last season. However, Luis Garcia's position as coach has come under question due to some woeful results of late. With Real Madrid in such great form, it is hard to see anything but a Madrid victory.
