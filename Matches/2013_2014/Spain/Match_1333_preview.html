    Prediction

    • Real Madrid made their first Champions League final in 12 years during the week with their incredible win over Bayern Munich. It suffices to say this is a team in red hot form and their star player, Ronaldo, is doing what he does best; scoring goals. The Portuguese has four in his last two games and he will be as hungry as ever when his side entertain Valencia.
    • Valencia usually pose a real threat for Real Madrid but their season has petered out recently. They sit some distance off the European places and with just one point in their last two they are hardly in great form. A trip to the Santiago Bernabeu will surely prove a task beyond Los Che.
    • For Real Madrid, nothing less than three points will do as they continue their bid for La Liga. A win looks likely too. Carlo Ancelotti has a full strength side to choose from and his team have been in irresistible form, winning their last five games in all competitions.
