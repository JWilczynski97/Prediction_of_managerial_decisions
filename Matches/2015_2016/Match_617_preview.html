    Team News

    Bayern Munich
    • Franck Ribery is a doubt for Bayern with a back problem and could miss out on a place in the starting XI.
    • Holger Badstuber and Arjen Robben are both ruled out with respective ankle and groin problems.
    • Thomas Muller may start here having been forced to settle for a place on the bench at the Calderon last week, with one of Thiago Alcantara or Arturo Vidal dropping out.
    Atletico Madrid
    • Atletico Madrid manager Diego Simeone is without Tiago who is ruled out through injury, but Diego Godin could feature alongside Gimenez at the heart of the defence.
    • Yannick Ferreira-Carrasco is fit after missing the first leg and should feature in some capacity.
    • Simeone could name the same XI that started last week's 1-0 win over Bayern.

    Prediction

    • Bayern fell to a 1-0 defeat in Madrid last week and were hold to a 1-1 draw by Borussia Monchengladbach at the weekend to put their Bundesliga title celebrations on hold. Indeed, Saturday's stalemate was only the second time they've failed to win at home this season.
    • Atletico's 1-0 win over Rayo Vallecano over the weekend saw them keep pace with La Liga's title chasing trio. Diego Simeone's side have won their last seven competitive outings and conceded just one goal in that time.
    • Bayern will have to work hard to break down Atletico's resolute defence, but the Spanish outfit could hold firm to secure their place in the Champions League final.
