    Atletico Madrid
    • Diego Godin joins Tiago on the sidelines for the welcome of Bayern, with the Uruguayan ruled out with a hamstring complaint for Atletico.

    • Holger Badstuber and Arjen Robben are the only players that missed training ahead of their Champions League clash against Atletico Madrid on Wednesday.

    Prediction

    • Atletico's resolute defence helped them progress to the semi finals of the Champions League at Barcelona's expense and it will provide the foundation once more to what could be a long evening for the La Liga side.     • The Bundesliga side are now favourites to win the Champions League and will look to exploit Godin's absence at the back. Current head coach Pep Guardiola has faced Atletico 10 times as a manager, winning eight of those match ups, and will hope his side can secure a first leg advantage on Wednesday.
    • Atletico, though, will be confident of securing a positive result despite Godin's absence, with a low score draw on the cards.
