    Team News

    FC Porto
    • Yacine Brahimi is a doubt, though is crucial to Porto's attack so could start this one.
    • Andre Andre and Vincent Aboubakar are expected to make up the midfield three when Chelsea visit.
    • Giannelli Imbula could line up in midfield after missing the 2-2 draw with Kyiv last time out in the Champions League.
    Chelsea
    • Thibaut Courtois is sidelined for the Premier League side, with Asmir Begovic retaining his spot in between the sticks.
    • Diego Costa is expected to lead the line, with the Spain international suspended from domestic duties until after the upcoming international break.
    • John Terry may come into the defence in place of Kurt Zouma, who was at fault for Ayoze Perez's goal in Saturday's 2-2 draw with Newcastle at the weekend.

    Prediction

    • FC Porto will have been buoyed by the commendable draw secured in Ukraine in gameweek 1. On home turf, and with Jose Mourinho returning to his former club, they will be keen to wrap up all three points in this one.
    • Chelsea romped to a 4-0 win over Maccabi Tel Aviv earlier this month, though the trip to Portugal will be that bit harder for the Blues to navigate. They will hope to maintain their unbeaten run on Tuesday night, which currently stands at four games.
    • Both teams are well matched, while Porto will hope to put on an impressive showing when Mourinho makes his return to the club. This could end in a draw.
