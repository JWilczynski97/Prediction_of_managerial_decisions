    Prediction

    • Arsenal against Manchester United has lost its spice in recent years, but there is still a lot riding on this fixture. Arsenal have a lot of credibility to earn back after their humilating 3-2 defeat to Olympiacos midweek and they will have to try and overcome the Red Devils for the first time in the Premier League since May 2011.
    • Louis van Gaal's go into gameweek 8 top of the table for the first real time during his tenure and it will be interesting to see how his side deal with the pressure that comes with such a position. United were fortunate to come away with all three points against Wolfsburg in midweek and will now be looking to build on their league form that has seen them score three goals in each of their last three matches.
    • Arsenal can draw level with United on 16 points with a win here, but a defeat would be a real dent to their chances to win the league back for the first time since 2004.
