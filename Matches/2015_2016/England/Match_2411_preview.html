    Team News

    Man City
    Leicester
    • Manuel Pellegrini could start in a 4-4-2 formation once more, with Kelechi Iheanacho partnering Sergio Aguero in attack as he looks to match Leicester's system.
    • The Chilean is without injured quartet Samir Nasri, Eliaquim Mangala, Wilfried Bony and Kevin de Bruyne, while Fabian Delph and Jesus Navas are both classed as doubts, but Vincent Kompany has returned to training.
    • Raheem Sterling could come into the starting XI to replace Navas, while Aleksandar Kolarov may also start if Pellegrini opts to rotate.
    • Jeffrey Schlupp and Matthew James are both sidelined once more for the Foxes, while Daniel Amartey is lacking match fitness may not be available for the trip to the Etihad Stadium.
    • Other than that, Claudio Ranieri has no further injury concerns, and so is expected to name the same side that overcame Liverpool on Tuesday night.

    Prediction

    • Manchester City maintained the pressure on league leaders Leicester City on Tuesday night with a 1-0 win at Sunderland, though the Citizens still sit three points behind the Foxes.
