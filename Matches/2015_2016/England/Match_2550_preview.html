    Prediction

    • Defeat to Arsenal, and Arsene Wenger in particular, would cap a terrible start to the season for Jose Mourinho. The reigning Premier League champions cannot afford to lose any more ground on Manchester City and will be desperate to overcome their London rivals.
    • There may not ever be a better time for Arsene Wenger to end his hoodoo over Jose Mourinho. The Arsenal boss has never triumphed against a Mourinho side in a competitive match and the two have never been shy of revealing their lack of love for one and other. By winning, Wenger would become just the third Premier League manager to record a league victory over Mourinho at Stamford Bridge.
    • Chelsea's midweek 4-0 win over Maccabi Tel Aviv should give them some confidence, whereas Arsenal will have to pick themselves up after a shock 2-1 defeat to Dinamo Zagreb midweek. Mourinho's side could play defensively and look to pick off some naive defending by the away side late on and sneak a close victory.
