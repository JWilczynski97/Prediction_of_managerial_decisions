    Team News

    Leicester
    Man City
    • Claudio Ranieri may be tempted to rest star men Jamie Vardy and Riyad Mahrez. Vardy played with a fever against Liverpool, whereas Ranieri admitted Mahrez was very tired.
    • The Leicester boss hasn't got too many options if he wants to rest Mahrez or Vardy, which may not lead to that many changes with Jeffrey Schlupp, Danny Drinkwater and Matt James all out.
    • Gokhan Inler is pushing for a change in the starting XI, although that doesn't look likely given N'Golo Kante's form.
