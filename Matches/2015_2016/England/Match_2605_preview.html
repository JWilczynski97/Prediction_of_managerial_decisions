    Team News

    Chelsea
    Southampton
    • With rumours continuing to mount of a dispute between John Terry and Jose Mourinho it remains to be seen whether the captain will return to the side.
    • Having scored in the week Willian may return to the side at the expense of Pedro, who was out of sorts against Porto.
    • With Diego Costa still suspended Loic Remy and Radamel Falcao are in competition to start up front having both been left out of the squad for the trip to Portugal.
    • It's been a good week for Ronald Koeman, who was able to welcome Ryan Bertrand back into the side last time out and now finally has summer signing Jordy Clasie at his disposal.
    • The Dutchman will likely be eased into action though, so changes are unlikely from the impressive win over Swansea last weekend.

    Prediction

    • Having seemingly turned a corner Chelsea are somewhat back to square one having drawn with Newcastle and lost at Porto in the space of a week.
    • Southampton, meanwhile, are finding some form and with the exception of Fraser Forster have all of their potential first team players available.
    • Both meetings between these two sides ended in stalemate last season, along with each of the visitors' 3 away games thus far this season, and another draw would be no great surprise this weekend.
