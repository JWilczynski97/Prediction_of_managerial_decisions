    Team News

    Juventus
    • Giorgio Chiellini is closing in on a return to fitness for Juventus, but has failed to make the squad for the welcome of Bayern Munich.
    • Elsewhere, Kwadwo Asamoah, Alex Sandro and Martin Caceres all miss out due to injury, though Mario Mandzukic is expected to start.
    • Max Allegri is expected to name his strongest XI after resting key players for the 0-0 draw with Bologna on Friday night.
    Bayern Munich
    • Pep Guardiola is without defenders Javi Martinez, Jerome Boateng and Holger Badstuber for the trip to Juventus.
    • Mehdi Benatia could come into the starting XI at centre-back, partnering David Alaba at the heart of the defence for this one, with Juan Bernat featuring at left-back.
    • Philipp Lahm and Thiago Alcantara are both expected to come into the side in place of Rafinha and Kingsley Coman

    Prediction

    • Last season's runners' up host Bayern Munich at the Juventus Stadium having failed to win any of their last four competitive meetings with the Bundesliga side, losing three. The Serie A outfit have lost the last two times these European heavyweights have met in Italy, with Bayern scoring a combined six goals in the process.
    • Bayern have failed to win only one of their last six competitive away games, that resulting in a 0-0 draw with Bayer Leverkusen. The German outfit lost one of their three away Champions League ties in the group stages, that coming at Arsenal, but should be confident of a positive result against the Serie A side.
    • This is a tough tie to call against two of Europe's in form sides, which could result in a draw.
