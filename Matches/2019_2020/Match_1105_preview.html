    Team News

    Juventus
    Lyon
    • Douglas Costa and Mattia De Sciglio miss the welcome of Lyon through injury, which could see Federico Bernardeschi handed a start on the right, though another option for Maurizio Sarri is to push Juan Cuadrado forward and have Danilo feature at right-back.
    • Paulo Dybala is a doubt, but he is expected to be fit to feature for the Italian giants, which is a major boost.
    • Matthijs de Ligt may continue in defence alongside Leonardo Bonucci.
    • Youssouf Kone misses the trip to Turin, while Kenny Tete and Marcelo will both be assessed.
    • Lucas Tousart scored the vital goal in the first leg, but has been sold to Hertha Berlin, but Rudi Garcia should continue with a three-man defence with Maxence Caqueret and Bruno Guimares featuring alongside Houssem Aouar in midfield.
    • Memphis Depay missed the first leg through injury, and his availability is a huge boost for the French side.

    Prediction

    • Juventus dominated possession in the first leg, but came unstuck by a Lucas Tousart goal on the half hour mark. Back on home turf, albeit with no fans in attendance, the Old Lady will look to draw level early on in order to ease pressure against Lyon.
    • Lyon have played just four competitive matches following the first leg due to the Coronavirus pandemic and recently lost the Coupe de la Ligue final to PSG on penalties. They have lost four of their last five away competitive matches coming into this tie and have failed to win their last two away European meetings with Juventus.
    • Juventus haven't been wholly impressive under Maurizio Sarri this season, but have the personnel to overturn the first leg loss.
