    Prediction

    • It's been a long time coming but Paris Saint-Germain have made their first ever Champions League final. To their credit they turned in a very accomplished performance against RB Leipzig in the semi-final and won 3-0. While they are underdogs heading into Sunday's final they are certainly by no means out of the running.
    • Bayern Munich have romped their way to the Champions League final and now the Bavarians have only their second ever European treble in sight. Bayern remarkably average 4.2 goals per game Europe this season, with Robert Lewandowski only two goals behind Cristiano Ronaldo's record of 17 in a single Champions League campaign.
    • Bayern clearly have the firepower to outscore PSG but they have looked very suspect at the back in both of their last two games. Neither Barcelona nor Lyon were able to capitalise on the space vacated behind Alphonso Davies at left-back but in Neymar, Angel Di Maria and Kylian Mbappe, PSG have just the tools to cause the German side major problems. This could prove one of the most entertaining finals in recent history.
