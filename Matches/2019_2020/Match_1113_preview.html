    Team News

    Lyon
    Bayern
    • Karl Toko Ekambi has surprisingly started Lyon's last two Champions League matches ahead of Moussa Dembele. The latter came off the bench and scored twice against Manchester City last week and now gives Rudi Garcia a major headache.
    • Other than that, no changes are expected from Lyon with 20-year-old Maxence Caqueret to remain in central midfield alongside fellow youngsters Bruno Guimaraes and Houssem Aouar.
    • Benjamin Pavard has returned to training ahead of Bayern Munich's semi-final with Lyon but Hansi Flick is not expected to tinker with the side that thrashed Barcelona 8-2 last time out. It means Joshua Kimmich will stay at right-back, with Thiago and Leon Goretzka continuing in central midfield.
    • Kingsley Coman is back fit but Ivan Perisic has done well in his absence and is expected to retain his place in the starting XI. Summer signing Leroy Sane cannot play for Bayern until the 2020/21 campaign starts.

    Prediction

    • Lyon dumped Manchester City out of the quarter-finals with a shock 3-1 victory to ruin any chance of Pep Guardiola facing former club Bayern Munich.
    • Bayern tore Barcelona to pieces in the quarter-final finals with an 8-2 victory that evoked memories of Germany's 7-1 thrashing of Brazil at the 2014 World Cup.
    • Bayern are no doubt the heavy favourites but Lyon cannot be underestimated, as they have knocked out Juventus and City in the last two rounds of the competition.
    • Even though Bayern showed against Barcelona they are vulnerable at the back, they surely have far too much firepower for Lyon to contain.
