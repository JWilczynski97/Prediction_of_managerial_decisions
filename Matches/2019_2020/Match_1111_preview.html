    Team News

    RBL
    PSG
    • Julian Nagelsmann is without the injury Ibrahima Konate for the semi-final meeting with PSG.
    • Hwang Hee-Chan and Benjamin Henrichs have joined RB Leipzig, but are ineligible to feature here.
    • Nagelsmann could name the same XI that started the 2-1 win over Atletico Madrid, which would see Christopher Nkunku face his former employers.
    • However, match winner Tyler Adams is pushing for a start, while Patrik Schick is also looking to lead the line, while Nordi Mukiele is an option should Nagelsmann wish to revert to a three-man defence.
    • Keylor Navas is a doubt for PSG having sustained a thigh injury that forced him off in the 2-1 win over Atalanta, with Sergio Rico in line to deputise.
    • Marco Verratti will also be assessed, but Layvin Kurzawa misses out.
    • Head coach Thomas Tuchel was able to call upon Kylian Mbappe from the bench against Atalanta and the Frenchman is in line to start here.
    • Angel Di Maria is also available following a ban and Tuchel could elect to use a 4-2-2-2 system with the Argentine and Neymar behind Mbappe and Mauro Icardi.

    Prediction

    • RB Leipzig left it late to secure their semi-final spot at Atletico Madrid's expense as Tyler Adams came off the bench to bag the winner two minutes from time. The German side proved they are able to match Europe's heavyweights without Timo Werner, which will have improved confidence.
    • PSG, too, secured their spot in the Champions League semi-final in the dying embers of their meeting with Atalanta. The French side are boosted by Angel Di Maria's availability following a ban and Kylian Mbappe's return to fitness, which swells Thomas Tuchel's attacking ranks considerably.
    • RB Leipzig will have taken confidence from their win over Atletico, but a meeting with PSG may be a step too far for the German side.
